About Us

Before Logistikana, businesses used to manually call multiple local delivery companies to find vehicles to delivery items to their customers. With Logistikana, vehicle owners use our app to instantly discover items and businesses get directly matched with vehicle owners.

Our online marketplace was created with a simple idea in mind: to make it easier for people to find and receive the items they need, delivered right to their doorstep. We believe that shopping for essential items should be fast, convenient, and hassle-free, which is why we've built a platform that makes it easy to get what you need, when you need it.

Our team is made up of passionate individuals who are dedicated to providing an exceptional delivery experience for our customers. From our customer support to our network of delivery partners, everyone at our marketplace is committed to making sure your orders arrive on time and in perfect condition.
